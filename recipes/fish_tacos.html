ingredients

  • tilapia, 1 fillet
  • some kind of beans or rice, 1/4 cup
  • cabbage slaw (find recipe for this mine always taste kinda funny)
  • mexican cheese, sprinkle
  • sour cream, dollop
  • cumin, a bit
  • qdoba seasoning, bit
  • oregano, bit+
  • cayenne pepper, enough to make it a nice spicy color
  • garlic powder, way too much

steps

  1. thaw the fish under some water
  2. start frying it up with a little oit in the pan
  3. mix together the spices with some more oil
  4. slather that shit up
  5. add toppings to perfection
  6. i forgot to say about you also need tortilla wraps, usually i use corn but it's whatever, i air fry it for like 2 minutes and it's fine.
